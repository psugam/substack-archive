Florilegium

Florilegium means ‘a gathering of flowers’ in Latin and was used in the past to mean collection of literary extracts, just like the Greek word it was calqued from ἀνθολογία (anthología) still means in English. I write about random things that I like or am interested in. There is no single theme or topic but my posts generally have to do with ancient or medieval languages and their corresponding literature.
